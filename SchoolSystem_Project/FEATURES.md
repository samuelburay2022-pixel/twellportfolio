# Digital Edu Pro School System Features

Status: Ongoing

## Core Modules

1. Role-based access and dashboards for Admin, ICT, TA, HRT, and Subject Teacher users.
2. Academic structure management: academic years, terms, MPR periods, term weeks, and holiday ranges.
3. Division-based setup (NK/Primary/Secondary/Senior High) with division calendars and grading profiles.
4. Curriculum setup: levels, classrooms, domains, and indicators.
5. Teacher management: create/edit teachers, roles, signatures, assignments, activate/deactivate assignments, CSV teacher import.
6. Student management (ICT): add/edit/delete/move students, CSV student import.
7. Weekly grade encoding via teacher grid, with configurable grading scales.
8. Final exam encoding and subject final-exam policy handling.
9. Encoding control system: term/month/week/MPR/class/teacher locks and term/exam encoding windows.
10. Reporting suite: MPR reports, term reports, HRT domain-student reports, admin class/domain reports.
11. Export features: per-student PDF, class PDF outputs, and batch ZIP of class term PDFs.
12. TA module: daily attendance (present/absent/late plus notes), term attendance summary, height/weight health records.
13. ICT attendance module: face enrollment, camera registry/ping, face/manual check-in/out events, recent events API, attendance CSV export.
14. School branding/profile management: school info, logo, login background, report signatory settings.
15. Activity logging and audit views for admin and ICT users.
16. Teacher print utilities: student list, daily scoring sheet, lesson plan, daily/weekly templates.

## Design Direction

Fully customizable to fit each school's needs and curriculum design.
