Digital Edu Pro School System

Ongoing Project

Web-based school operations platform that unifies academic setup, grading, attendance, reporting, and administration through role-based workflows. Designed for flexibility so each school can align the system to its own curriculum, calendars, policies, and report standards.

Feature Groups

System scope by module

Access and Core Setup

  • Role-based access for Admin, ICT, TA, HRT, and Subject Teacher users.
  • Academic years, terms, MPR periods, term weeks, and holiday ranges.
  • Division-based setup for NK, Primary, Secondary, and Senior High.
  • School branding and profile configuration.

Curriculum and People

  • Curriculum setup with levels, classrooms, domains, and indicators.
  • Teacher management, signatures, assignments, and CSV teacher import.
  • Student lifecycle workflows for ICT including move/import tools.
  • Assignment activation/deactivation controls.

Grading and Reporting

  • Weekly grading via teacher grid with configurable grading scales.
  • Final exam encoding with subject final-exam policy handling.
  • Encoding lock controls and term/exam encoding windows.
  • MPR, term, and domain-level reporting for different user roles.

Attendance, Exports, and Audit

  • TA attendance, term summaries, and health record tracking.
  • ICT face/manual check-in workflows and attendance CSV exports.
  • Per-student PDF, class PDF, and batch ZIP export tools.
  • Activity logging and audit trails for Admin and ICT.
Customization Goal Fully customizable to fit each school's needs and curriculum design.
